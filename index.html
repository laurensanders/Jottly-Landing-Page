So what is Jott.ly?

Jott.ly is all about simplicity, allowing our users an easier, more enjoyable experience when collaborating on projects and ideas. Simply write down your thoughts and Jott.ly will save, store, and organize them for quick access at a later time. With the click of a button, you can easily give accesss to coworkers, siblings, your boss, or friends to view and even edit your content.
